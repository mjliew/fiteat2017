What will happen if I cancel my meal package before it ends?

This depends on the number of days left.

  • More than 30 days:
    50% of the ordered inital meal package cost.
  • Less than 14 days:
    20% of the ordered inital meal package cost.
  • Less than 7 days:
    8% of the ordered inital meal package.
  • Less than 24 hours:
    Penalty fee of 10 Euros.
